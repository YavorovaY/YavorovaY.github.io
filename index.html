About

I am a PhD candidate affiliated with the Department of Internal Medicine at Radboudumc and the Department of Cell Biology at Radboud University. I am part of the research group of Prof. Musa Mhlanga, who serves as my primary supervisor, with Prof. Mihai Netea as co-supervisor. My research interests lie at the intersection of molecular and cell biology, trained immunity, epigenetics, and mitochondrial biology. I am particularly interested in understanding how mitochondrial biology shapes innate immune memory across health and disease. My work combines computational analysis with experimental laboratory research, allowing me to integrate multi-omics, imaging, and molecular approaches to study immune regulation across scales.
